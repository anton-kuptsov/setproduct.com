<span class="blog\_big-paragraph">Being an independent founder, you may be in the know that when starting a SaaS company, a person's purpose is to make it the cheapest way possible. Given the many options that are now on the market, it is very difficult to select the service that will suit your budget perfectly well.</span>

In this article, we will look at all the services offered by a founder, which can give you certain assistance in saving a big sum of money when launching SaaS.

When you start a SaaS business, it is important to break its components into easily manageable parts that are less in size. This includes front-end hosting, server infrastructure, authentication and database, analytics, customer support, and some security measures such as Captcha.

We will take a detailed look at each of these components and analyze the services that will help you run SaaS without any even the slightest money investment.

**TL/DR**

1.  For front-end hosting, apply [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com/) to use its unlimited bandwidth and a free plan for the best of your work.
2.  For back-end infrastructure, try [Digital Ocean](https://www.digitalocean.com/) and take advantage of a free 2-month $200 credit in order to begin your work.
3.  For databases and authentication, select [Supabase](https://supabase.com/), but be aware of the free tier restriction period.
4.  For customer support, apply [Crisp](https://crisp.chat/) to ensure the best experience process for those who will use it.
5.  For more convenient analytics, choose [Plausible](https://plausible.io/) over Google Analytics.
6.  For a free and easy integration of a security solution, turn to [hCaptcha](https://www.hcaptcha.com/).
7.  For a more flexible solution with a good price, use Self-host with [Coolify](https://coolify.io/).
8.  For creating your brand and identity, apply [Namelix](https://namelix.com/) in order to save both money and time resources.

## Front-end Hosting: Cloudflare Pages

It's quite practical for any developer to know that [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com/) is the perfect platform if you want to host a certain client-side application.

Since there is a free plan with an unlimited bandwidth, which will undoubtedly save you a considerable sum of money when time for monthly bills comes. This platform for front-end is secure and provides good scalability, which, in turn, gives unlimited space for creativity of indie makers. 

Cloudflare Pages offers a free plan which contains such features as custom domains, deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, continuous integration and SSL encryption.

## Back-end Infrastructure: Digital Ocean

A platform with the name that speaks for itself – [Digital Ocean](https://www.digitalocean.com/) – seems to be a widely spread choice with independent server infrastructure developers everywhere.

Frankly speaking, you save a lot if you rent a Linux VM (virtual machine) or "dropper" with them. No other cloud providers make an offer that is even half as good.

At first sight it may look like setting up a droplet is an unachievable task, but you may choose between a plenty of guides available online to give you a hand in the process. What is even more attractive, there is a free two-month loan of $200, which gives you a chance to see how DigitalOcean works and make your own conclusions without paying money.

## Database and Authentication: Supabase

The next platform, [Supabase](https://supabase.com/), is a PostgreSQL-based database that operates as a service and provides a free tier, which makes it an ideal solution for running SaaS.

Supabase can easily be integrated into an app as it has a user-friendly interface that is quite simple for using. Such features as authentication and authorization, as well as real-time data synchronization are a part of the free tier, making it truly useful for you.

But bear in mind that in case you need a NoSQL database, you'd better search for a different, more suitable solution.

## Customer Support: Crisp

Speaking of customer support platforms, I\`d call [Crisp](https://crisp.chat/) a reasonable solution since there is a free tier right for timing and launching a successful SaaS company.

This platform offers a smooth support service for the users of your app and the integration process is rather simple as it needs only one line of code to get it into.

Such features as website analytics, a message box and a dashboard that can be adjusted to your requirements are a few of its advantages.

## Analytics: Google Analytics vs. Plausible

The platform that is the first option that comes to mind when we deal with analytics, is, surely, Google Analytics. But try to widen your horizons and have a closer look at [Plausible](https://plausible.io/). It features a really user-friendly interface that has won the hearts of many indie developers.

The price is just $10, but the range of features includes referrers, bounce rates and page views. So, this platform is an ideal version for those who search for a simple solution that is easy to use.

## Captcha: hCaptcha

Can you imagine creating your app without a captcha? Hardly.

A captcha data verification solution that provides reliable protection, is free and can easily be integrated into your app is [hCaptcha](https://www.hcaptcha.com/).

You may have heard of reCaptcha, which is well-known nowadays, but hCaptcha is no way worse in terms of security as well as the price options. Spam and abuse are effectively prevented when you integrate it into your app.

## Self-hosting: Coolify

In case self-hosting is your favorite option, go for [Coolify](https://coolify.io/).

If you do, all your projects and resources will effectively be managed from just one dashboard on this free platform with an open source. Besides, such tasks as CI/CDwill be automated, which is one more advantage.

Coolify copies the capabilities of Vercel/Heroku in a much less expensive way, providing you with an opportunity to be as flexible as you wish and customize your solutions.

One of Coolify\`s big pluses is that you can scale up your projects, starting a SaaS business with the simplest and going up to more complex solutions, even load-balancing.

## Name, Brand, Colors: Namelix

I have no doubt you\`ve already heard of [Namelix](https://namelix.com/) as a cool resource for branding, but be sure to give it a try.

It\`s advantages include such options as finding a name, creating your brand identity, as well as choosing your SaaS colors.

So, judge for yourself: it saves your time as it takes minutes to get started, and it saves your money as it has everything you need for branding without a need to search for additional features. With Namelix, the branding process has turned into an enjoyable experience!

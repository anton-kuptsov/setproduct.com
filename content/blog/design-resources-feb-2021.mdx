## Free Emoji Pack

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-1.jpg)

[**https://www.figma.com/community/file/937774188065101204**](https://www.figma.com/community/file/937774188065101204)

‍[Free Emoji Pack](https://www.figma.com/community/file/937774188065101204) is a great collection of emoji pictures for any project. The pack consists of over 1,700 emojis that are conveniently categorized, well-structured and easy to use. Collections like these will always be a great addition and bring some creativity and fun into your project.

All pictures are raster and represent the following categories: Smilies, People, Animals and Nature, Food and Drink, Travel and Places, Objects, Symbols, Flags.

## Illustration Kit

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-2.jpg)

[**https://illustrationkit.com**](https://illustrationkit.com/)

You can never have too many good illustrations! [Illustration Kit](https://illustrationkit.com/) is a brand new resource with new and free illustrations added every day in SVG, Figma and Sketch formats.

You can use them for both personal and commercial purposes.

This pack has almost 60 characters and 120+ scenes that can be used in various projects.

You can change the color scheme of the illustrations (to match your brand) - both the skin-tone and colors.

You can also track the progress of adding items on the website by going to the Track Progress page. There, you can see which illustrations will be added soon, which are under development, and which have already been added.

## Akar Icons

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-4.jpg)

[**https://akaricons.com**](https://akaricons.com/)

‍[Akar Icons](https://akaricons.com/) is a great library of free and nicely rounded icons that can be used for a wide variety of projects.

## Color within Constraints

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-5.jpg)

[**https://medium.com/tap-to-dismiss/color-within-constraints-d6f777a3b72d**](https://medium.com/tap-to-dismiss/color-within-constraints-d6f777a3b72d)

## Morph

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-7.jpg)

[**https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/906950256777348534/Morph**](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/906950256777348534/Morph)

‍[Morph](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/906950256777348534/Morph) is a must-have plugin if you want cool styling in Figma.  
  
With this plugin, you can create cool and trendy effects such as skeuomorphism, neon, glitch, reflection, glass, etc.  
  
Using the plugin is very simple: select any object or text layer, choose a transform effect, then get the desired result!  
  
Effects are customizable, each with its own properties that are very easy to control.

## StyleList

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-8.jpg)

[**https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/927255248672920500/StyleList**](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/927255248672920500/StyleList-%E2%80%93-text-and-color-styles)

If you face difficulties creating text styles, then [this powerful plugin](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/927255248672920500/StyleList-%E2%80%93-text-and-color-styles) will make your life easier.

StyleList will create text styles and color styles (under development) for you, significantly saving you time.

And this plugin is also very easy to use: Activate it → Set the desired values → Click on the "Create Styles" button → Enjoy life

## Angel Match

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-9.jpg)

[**https://angelmatch.io**](https://angelmatch.io/)

The website [Angel Match](https://angelmatch.io/) is a constantly updated database of over 50,000 investors. Thanks to this resource, the long and exhausting procedure of finding business angels and venture capitalists have become much easier.

You can search for investors for your business by location, company, investment interests, and past investments.

You can add the investors to your "Favorite list" and contact them later.

And for even greater convenience, the list can be exported in CSV format.

## Vector Pattern Generator

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-10.jpg)

[**https://www.visiwig.com/patterns**](https://www.visiwig.com/patterns/)

Pattern generators seem to be trending. The Visiwig resource provides another good [vector pattern generator](https://www.visiwig.com/patterns/).

Adjust the scale of a pattern, rotate it to any degree, change the color, as well as the width of the stroke - the tool is very flexible.

You can export the pattern to CSS and SVG.

## Tweek Calendar

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-12.jpg)

[**https://tweek.so**](https://tweek.so/)

‍[Tweek Calendar](https://tweek.so/) is a great weekly planning and to-do list app.

Tweek is designed as a weekly calendar with no hourly scheduling. The creators believe that this look will relieve stress and increase productivity.

What's available:  

-   Reminders - sent to you by email.
-   Stickers and color themes - let you highlight important things.
-   Synchronization with Google Calendar
-   Availability for PC and mobile devices: iPhone and Android.
-   Notes, checklists and subtasks

## Uppbeat

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-14.jpg)

[**https://uppbeat.io**](https://uppbeat.io/)

The top three are opened by [Uppbeat](https://uppbeat.io/). It's a cool and growing resource for creatives looking for free music for videos, podcasts, and more. No copyright claims.

## Text Gradients

![](/blog/assets/design-resources-feb-2021/img-15.jpg)

[**https://textgradients.com**](https://textgradients.com/)

In second place we have [Text Gradients](https://textgradients.com/). This resource is intended to create a gradient for the text and immediately receive CSS code for it.

We invite you to [submit a guest post](https://publish.setproduct.com/) to our blog. For a do-follow link we charge $88+.  
No follow backlinks are for free, however we moderate incoming posts seriously.

We share web and graphic design ideas, inspiration tutorials as well as useful resources.

Our blog appeals to graphic & web designers and developers, small business owners, freelancers yet those creative-minded.

## Why Write for Us?

There are several benefits to writing for our blog:

-   Author credit for the post content.
-   Add a short bio at the end of your post.
-   Access to one of the largest audiences in design and development.
-   Being associated with a strong, established brand in the industry.

## Topics of Interest to Us

We accept guest post submissions on any design/development topic:

-   **Graphic design**: techniques, tutorials, tools, and inspiration.
-   **Website design and development**: guides, best practices, frameworks, coding tips, and case studies.
-   **Video design and creation**: tutorials for editing, animation, software reviews, and interesting ideas.
-   **SEO**: comprehensive strategy, tools, and recent updates.
-   **Social media and other digital marketing**: tendencies, strategies, tools, and case studies.

## Guest Posting Guidelines

![Guest Posting Guidelines](/blog/assets/submit-a-guest-post/img-1.jpg)

We look forward to your submissions, welcome to the blog! Please follow these guest post submission guidelines to maintain the quality of our content and provide value to our readers by submitting a guest post:

**1\. Original and Non-plagiarized Content**: Your post has to be 100% original, non-plagiarized content on our blog. Plagiarism or duplication will be rejected. 

**2\. Relevant Topics**: The content should be something that we consider relevant to our niche, such as graphic design, web design, website development, SEO, social media marketing, internet marketing, or other technology niches. 

**3\. Article Length**: Minimum 1000 words or more for an article. There is no exact upper word limit, but try to be somewhere between 1000 to 2000 words to provide some good amount and valuable content. No fluff, repetition.

**4\. AI-Generated Content Policy**: We do not support obviously AI-created content. However, we are not against AI content that was rewritten and edited like human text after it has been generated as long as  it meets our quality standards.

**5\. Quality Standards**: A post should be well-written, grammatically flawless, and contain original, useful, organized helpful content. Write in an approachable, conversational style that is both entertaining and easy-to-read.

**6\. Structure with Headings**: Use headings, subheadings, and short paragraphs to improve readability. Use an uppercase letter for headings.

**7\. Links**: Up to 2 relevant outbound links to your company website or related resources are appropriate within the body. These links bring value and relevance to your content but should not include affiliate links, shortened URLs, naked URLs, or vanity URLs. All the links must be “nofollow”, unless they are to an authoritative site. Links to casinos, essays, sports betting, gambling, CBD, dating, or other inappropriate topics will not work.

**8\. Citations and Attribution**: All facts, figures, quotes, and photos must be properly attributed. You must cite your sources as proof of credibility and transparency.

**9\. Images**: Supporting images with appropriate attribution may be included to complement the content and make the post more visually appealing. However, the images should not be stock images; instead, they should be highly original or from accredited sources. A featured image for the post is necessary.

**10\. Banned Content**: Your article is not allowed to be inferred or contain references/links with dating, gambling, pornography/violence/racism, and other taboo topics. We also do not approve CBD, adult content, loans or payday, cryptos, vaping, etc.

**11\. Submission Format**: Please submit your paper as a Word.doc. docx, or Google Doc format. I had to send all the images as separate files. Please provide your desired Title and Meta Description tags in the submission.

**12\. Authorship and Bio**: At the end of your post you can insert a short author bio (1-2 sentences) at the bottom with links to your own website or social media. Even include a cv with an image of the author to profile, must be present.

**13\. Editorial Rights**: We retain all rights to change or modify the submission's content, we own it. We have the right to reject any post that does not adhere to our criteria, or for the reason of our choosing. Additionally, we reserve the right to eliminate outgoing links which are considered harmful for our search rankings or if its linked content changes considerably.

**14\. How to Submit**: Please do not submit by email. Submit your post using our guest post application form below.

We invite you to submit your guest posts and become an author on our blog!

## How to Submit a Guest Post

We appreciate quality content and encourage you to contribute it to our blog. Be sure to follow the guest posting instructions as outlined for submission:

**Submission Process**: Please do not email your articles; instead fill out the submit form on this link. Just submit your post through our guest post from the button below.

**Submission Guidelines**: Submit your guest post adhering to our guest post guidelines so we can accept it for sure. This also means following specified topics, meeting a word count, linking properly, and attributing correctly.

**Links to Existing Blog Posts**: We will also consider high-quality resource link placements within our current network of blog posts. If you have a link that is pertinent regarding the content we are already posting, submit for review. The resource must be a valuable resource for our readers.

We are excited to receive your guest post and work with you on providing the best content for our community. Begin by filling out the form, or click on that little button to submit a guest blog post.

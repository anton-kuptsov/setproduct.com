Video content creation has become the actual cornerstone of any online presence. Given that video makes up a stunning 82.5% of all web traffic (read that again, ALL web traffic) it practically means your online presence does not exist if you’re not investing in video of some kind. It’s become clear as day - no video, no traffic. 

Add to that 3.5 billion viewers of digital video on the planet and an average of 19 hours viewing time weekly, and the result is a sky high demand for engaging and high-quality video content. The question is, how do you meet that demand?

All that means one more thing - the need for innovation in video creation has become the thing that sets you apart in that sea of visual content. Let’s go over innovative video ideas for your next production.

## Choosing The Right Video Production Type

All right, so where do we start? Well, from the beginning and in this case it’s the type of video production you actually need. If you’re a small restaurant looking to get your name out there your video production needs are not the same as those of, say, Nike.

We’ll explore how to go through choosing the right video production type,  and to do that we’ll go over the 3 most important factors:understanding your needs, evaluating the company’s portfolio and style, and considering their technology and equipment.

### Understanding Your Needs And Goals

How can you get what you need if you don’t know what you need? We’ll you don’t, and it’s the same with your creative video design and production needs. Once you know what you want to achieve you’ll the road to your video content will be clear.

#### Clarify Your Objectives

Before selecting a video production type, you absolutely need to define your goals. What exactly are you striving to achieve? Brand awareness, promote a product, or tell a compelling story? What is the scope of your video project? How much money can you spare? Do you need a [videographer or a video production company](https://www.slinkyproductions.co.uk/videographer-comparison/)?

Knowing your primary objectives helps in selecting a production type that fits your vision like a glove. Promotional videos, like Apple's iPhone 15 launch, highlight new features through captivating narratives, combining stunning visuals, music, and narration to create a mesmerizing experience​​. 

Naturally, we don’t expect you to have the exuberant budget Apple has but the principle stays the same. 

#### Identify Your Audience

Who is your video for? You don’t make the same types of video content for Gen Z and your dear grandmother who just got her head around this internet thing. Different demographics respond better to types of videos fit for them. 

For instance, Nike’s “[A Feel For Every You](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GVljFVClyws)” campaign targets a diverse audience of women passionate about fitness, using visually stunning and relatable content​​. 

### Evaluating Portfolio And Style

Reviewing a company's past work provides valuable insights into their creative process, capabilities and style.

#### Review Past Work

Examine the portfolios of potential video production companies to see if their previous projects align with your brand’s tone and style.

Look for examples that showcase the type of video you need. 

Bolt’s corporate video effectively captures the company’s innovative spirit, positioning itself as a leader in the tech industry through stunning visuals and compelling narratives​​.

#### Assess Range of Work

A diverse portfolio indicates versatility and creativity. Companies with experience across various video types bring creative video ideas and a broader skill set to your project.

Booking.com’s humorous video featuring Idris Elba is a great example of a company using a unique approach to engage their audience while delivering key messages​​. Once again, we don’t expect you to be able to afford Idris Elba’s time but the idea is the same. In case you can afford his time, well done!

### Considering Technology And Equipment

Now this is a tough one, since presumably, you don’t know much about video production let alone the tech and equipment involved. Fear not, since there is some advice that’ll get you through the thick and thin of video production.

#### Latest Technology

It really seems everybody and their next door neighbor would advise this but it is important. Make sure the company you choose uses up-to-date equipment and software. 

Advanced cameras, lighting, and post-production tools can do the quality of your video a world of good. High-definition cameras and professional lighting make a world of difference in visual appeal, while advanced editing software adds creative effects and polish​​.

#### Post-Production Capabilities

This stage is when your video comes to life through editing, color grading, and sound design.  More than anything else, make sure that the company has strong post-production capabilities to ensure a high-quality final product. 

Ensuring the inclusion of professional editing and sound design, as seen in high-budget Kickstarter videos, can elevate the production quality and audience engagement​​.

## Mastering Advanced Video Editing Techniques

We’re out of the basic woods and in we go into advanced woods. Video editing can transform your raw footage into a compelling narrative your audience interacts with. 

Now we’ll brush over advanced video editing techniques, emphasizing visual effects, color grading, and transitions, along with real-world examples relevant to internet video content creation. 

### Visual Effects And CGI

Incorporating visual effects (VFX) and computer-generated imagery (CGI) can impact a video's storytelling and visual appeal in a very real manner, and for internet content creators it is an absolute must.

#### Enhancing Realism

Visual effects and CGI allow editors to create realistic settings and scenarios that enrich the visual experience for the target audience.

Take [Corridor Digital](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCsn6cjffsvyOZCZxvGoJxGg) for example, a YouTube channel known for its VFX-heavy content, uses effects to create videos with fantastical scenarios that keep viewers coming back. They use Adobe After Effects to add explosions, weather effects, and other elements that make their videos more immersive and engaging​​. Once again, it’s in the principle not in the actual explosions or weather effects. If you make handmade soaps, for instance, we don’t expect you to force explosions into your videos. 

#### Seamless Integration

To maintain continuity and avoid distractions, it's crucial to integrate VFX and CGI seamlessly with live-action footage. 

This requires skillful editing and a thorough understanding of software capabilities. Tools like Adobe After Effects and Blender are essential for achieving smooth and cohesive visual effects. Film Riot, another popular YouTube channel, provides tutorials on integrating VFX into live footage effectively​​.

### Advanced Color Grading And Correction

Color grading and correction are vital for creating the desired atmosphere and enhancing the visual storytelling of your video.

#### Color Manipulation and Adjustment Layers

Utilize adjustment layers and advanced color manipulation tools to apply targeted color adjustments, creating videos with visually distinct effects in your footage.

This technique helps in maintaining visual consistency and achieving a specific look throughout the video. YouTubers like Peter McKinnon use these techniques to give their travel vlogs a cinematic quality​​.

#### LUTs and Custom Color Presets

Look-Up Tables (LUTs) and custom color presets streamline the color grading process. They enable editors to apply consistent color tones across multiple shots, ensuring a cohesive visual style. 

LUTs are particularly useful for creating cinematic looks and maintaining a professional quality in the final output. Creators like Sam Kolder offer LUTs that others can use to achieve similar visual styles in their videos​​.

### Implementing Unique Transitions And Montages

Once again, the field is a bit technical but it will involve common sense and you’ll get the hang of what you’re looking for.

#### Unique Transitions

Transitions such as cuts, dissolves, and wipes are your best friends and will help create a sense of continuity and flow between scenes.

The match cuts used by YouTube video creators like Casey Neistat are a pretty good example of how these techniques work to bring more to your video.

#### Montages

Montages allow for time compression and can showcase a series of events or actions engagingly. 

  
A well-crafted montage can depict a character's progression, highlight key moments, or build up to a climactic event. 

Creators like Daniel Schiffer use montages to showcase product features and create dynamic, engaging content.

## Leveraging Advanced Animation Techniques For Video Content Marketing

Now we’ll go from advanced wood into advanced woods of a different type. Your video content might or might not benefit from animation but we would be remiss not to go over it. Animation, if done right,  can make your videos stand out in a heartbeat. As before we don’t except you to turn into a video or an animation expert but it would be great if you went over some of the basics.  

Let’s take a look at  advanced animation techniques, focusing on motion graphics, character animation, and 3D animation, with practical advice for marketers.

### Motion Graphics

We are aware this article is growing ever more technical, but motion graphics are a must for explainer videos, product demos, and social media content.

#### Utilizing Minimalist Color Schemes

A minimalist color scheme can make your motion graphics stand out by emphasizing simplicity and clarity. This approach helps in delivering your message effectively without overwhelming the viewer and as the name implies it’s not going to take too much of your time to choose something that features a minimalist color scheme.

Brands like Apple and Google often use minimalist designs in their motion graphics to create a clean and professional look. You must know this by heart by now, but don't think about  the scope but rather about the princple. 

Using tools like Adobe After Effects, you can create sleek animations with limited colors and ample white space, ensuring your message is both clear and visually appealing​​.

#### Mixing 2D and 3D Elements

Yet more technical stuff, but we in you, you can power through this. Since animation can be 2D or 3D this also merits mention. Combining 2D and [3D animation](https://www.setproduct.com/blog/how-to-3d-animate-scroll) elements can create a unique visual experience.

You might use 2D characters interacting with 3D objects or environments, which adds depth and interest to your videos. 

This technique is popular in product animations and marketing videos where brands aim to showcase their products in a dynamic and visually compelling manner. 

Software like Blender or Cinema 4D can be used to seamlessly integrate these elements​​.

### Character Animation

If your brand has a  character it is absolutely essential that you give it life by animating it. If it doesn’t this might inspire you to think of one, as we’ll go into below. 

#### Crafting Relatable Characters

Creating characters that reflect your brand's personality and values can help build a connection with your audience. It basically gives a face to the message you want to send. 

Mailchimp's animated characters in their explainer videos are designed to be friendly and approachable, aligning with the brand's user-friendly image. 

Tools like Toon Boom Harmony or Adobe Character Animator allow you to create and animate characters that can represent your brand effectively​​.

#### Integrating Voiceover and Lip Sync

Adding voice overs and lip-syncing your animated characters can make your videos more engaging and informative. 

This technique is essential for explainer videos, tutorials, and customer service animations. Software like Adobe Character Animator allows you to sync your character's lip movements with recorded dialogue, enhancing the realism and impact of your animations​​.

### 3D Animation

3D animation is such an important field that even though we’ve mentioned combining it with 2D we also need to give it a separate section.  The depth and realism to your creative videos that it gives, makes it special mention.

#### Creating Detailed Product Animations

Here’s your ticket to conveying your message clearly and very precisely.. 

This technique allows you to showcase your product from all angles (this is make or break), highlighting features and functionalities in a visually engaging manner. Think explainer videos. 

### Implementing Realistic Textures And Lighting

To enhance the realism of your 3D animations, focus on realistic textures and lighting. This approach can make your animated videos look more lifelike and professional. 

Once again, [Blender](https://www.blender.org/) and Autodesk Maya offer advanced tools for texturing and lighting, allowing you to create high-quality 3D animations that stand out. 

This technique is widely used in marketing videos for automotive, tech, and luxury brands​​.

## Wrapping Up 

Embracing advanced video editing and animation techniques not only enhances the visual appeal of your content but also deepens storytelling, ensuring a stronger connection with your audience. 

Staying updated with the latest tools and trends in innovative video production allows for more innovative and engaging video marketing strategies.

As video content continues to dominate the digital landscape, leveraging these techniques will be crucial for standing out and effectively communicating your brand message.

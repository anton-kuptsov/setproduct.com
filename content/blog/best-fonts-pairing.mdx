## The best fonts pairing for 2023 nominated by Setproduct

<span class="blog\_big-paragraph">2023 is set to be continued full of bold and creative fonts in graphic design. We are constantly looking for the latest and greatest font pairings to enhance our visuals. Two of the most popular picks for font pairings are Spline Sans and Outfit.</span>

[**Spline Sans**](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Spline+Sans) is a modern sans-serif typeface with a unique and eye-catching aesthetic. Its geometric shapes and rounded edges create a unique look that is perfect for branding, print materials, web & apps design. Spline Sans is nicely accessible and easy to read. Fits ideally to use as a body text.[**‍**](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit)

[**Outfit**](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit) is a geometric sans-serif typeface with a modern and sophisticated look. Its strong and bold shapes make it a great choice for headers that need a bit of a punch. This trendy neo-grotesk font is crafted perfectly, well shaped, and always on our UI design radar.  

![Best fonts combination for 2023 in graphic design](/blog/assets/best-fonts-pairing/img-1.jpg)

These two fonts work together very well, creating a strong and modern look that is perfect for any graphic design project. They are free fonts, available at [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) and licensed as [OFL](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SIL_Open_Font_License). This makes them the perfect choice for any designer looking to create an eye-catching design without breaking the bank.
